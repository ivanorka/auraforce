# Pricing And Usage

## Pricing Principle

Do not sell unlimited AI.

The product should combine platform fee, setup fee and usage-based revenue.

## Example Platform Plans

| Plan | Monthly Fee |
|---|---:|
| Starter | `$499-$1,000` |
| Growth | `$1,500-$3,500` |
| Pro | `$5,000-$12,000` |
| Enterprise | Custom |

## Example Setup Fees

| Plan | Setup Fee |
|---|---:|
| Starter | `$2,000-$5,000` |
| Growth | `$5,000-$20,000` |
| Pro | `$20,000-$75,000` |
| Enterprise | `$75,000+` |

## Metered Usage

Meter these items from day one:

- AI tokens
- model routing tier
- voice minutes
- call recordings
- WhatsApp volume
- SMS volume
- email volume
- storage
- premium integrations

## Margin Control

Usage should include margin, not only pass-through cost.

The platform should support:

- monthly included credits
- top-ups
- auto top-ups
- usage alerts
- hard caps
- per-company limits
- per-AI-employee limits
- provider cost reporting
