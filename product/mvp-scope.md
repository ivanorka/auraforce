# MVP Scope

## MVP Goal

Launch a revenue-generating real estate AI sales platform for Dubai agencies.

## Core Promise

Every real estate inquiry gets answered, qualified, scheduled and tracked automatically, with human handoff when needed.

## Include In Phase 1

- multi-tenant company workspaces
- user roles and permissions
- CRM and lead pipeline
- lead capture
- unified inbox for WhatsApp, email and web chat
- AI Receptionist
- AI Sales Rep
- appointment booking
- calendar integration
- lead follow-up sequences
- basic sales automation
- owner dashboard
- admin panel
- usage tracking
- billing and top-up logic
- real estate workflow template

## Consider Carefully For Phase 1

- inbound AI voice
- call transcripts
- call recordings
- advanced reporting
- premium AI employee builder

These may be included only if pilot sales require them and the budget supports them.

## Defer

- cold calling
- full AI call centre
- HR module
- document signing
- website builder
- vehicle tracking
- native mobile apps
- advanced marketing suite
- law, clinic, recruitment and car rental templates

## MVP Success Metrics

- lead response time
- qualified lead count
- booked viewing count
- follow-up completion
- recovered missed inquiries
- handoff quality
- AI usage cost per account
- agency willingness to pay
