# Usage And AI Governance

AI usage must be controlled technically and commercially from day one.

## What To Track

Track usage by company, user, channel and AI employee:

- AI tokens
- model tier
- call minutes
- WhatsApp messages
- SMS
- email volume
- storage
- call recordings
- number of users
- number of clients / leads
- automation runs

## Typical Token Planning

AI chat:

- simple conversation: 2,000-5,000 tokens
- lead qualification flow: 5,000-15,000 tokens
- long support conversation: 10,000-30,000 tokens

AI voice call:

- typical call length: 3-5 minutes
- estimated token usage: 5,000-20,000 tokens
- plus telephony and voice provider costs

Monthly scale examples:

| Active Clients | Estimated Monthly Tokens |
|---:|---:|
| 100 | 5M-20M |
| 1,000 | 50M-200M |
| 10,000 | 500M-2B+ |

## Wallet And Top-Up Flow

Each client should see:

- monthly plan
- included AI usage
- included call minutes
- included messages
- current balance
- top-up button
- auto top-up option
- usage history
- warning before limits

Example product messages:

- "You have 120 call minutes left."
- "You have used 80% of your WhatsApp credits."
- "Top up $100 / $250 / $500."
- "Auto top-up when balance drops below $50."

## Limit Handling

The system should not fail suddenly when limits are reached.

When a client approaches a limit:

- warn the client
- notify admins
- pause non-critical automations
- allow top-up
- recommend plan upgrade
- block expensive actions only when needed

## AI Learning Principle

The AI should improve over time, but not by learning freely without control.

Good improvement sources:

- reviewed conversations
- user feedback
- successful sales scripts
- lead outcome tracking
- FAQs
- call summaries
- objection handling
- approved answers

Governance rule:

AI suggests improvements. Humans approve them. Approved knowledge becomes part of the company AI brain.

## Data Access Rule

Each AI employee should only access the data needed for its job.

Examples:

- AI Receptionist can read lead intake scripts and create lead records.
- AI Sales Rep can see lead history and follow-up tasks.
- AI Meeting Assistant can read meeting notes and create summaries.
- AI Call Centre Agent follows stricter scripts, consent and escalation rules.

This protects clients and makes the AI easier to trust.
