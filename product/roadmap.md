# Roadmap

## Phase 1 - Real Estate MVP

Budget:

- lean build scenario: `$35k-$75k` offshore, `$80k-$150k` UK/EU
- recommended commercial budget: `$150k-$300k`

Timeline: 8-12 weeks lean MVP, or 3-5 months for stronger commercial V1

Build:

- SaaS foundation
- CRM
- lead management
- unified inbox
- AI Receptionist
- AI Sales Rep
- booking and calendar
- basic automation
- owner dashboard
- admin panel
- usage tracking
- billing and top-ups
- real estate workflows

## Phase 2 - Stronger AI Platform

Budget: `+$150k-$350k`, with a leaner SaaS foundation path possible if scope is controlled

Timeline: 3-5 additional months

Add:

- inbound AI calls
- stronger sales automation
- AI Meeting Assistant
- reporting
- AI employee templates
- better onboarding
- reputation management
- more integrations

## Phase 3 - Multi-Niche Expansion

Budget: `+$300k-$800k+`

Timeline: 6-12 additional months

Add:

- cold calling
- AI call centre
- mobile apps
- HR
- document signing
- marketing suite
- car rental template
- recruitment template
- law firm template
- clinic template
- vehicle tracking
- website builder

## App Strategy

Build web-first.

Start with:

- responsive web app
- mobile-friendly dashboard
- PWA if needed

Delay full native iOS and Android apps until the core platform is proven and the team knows which daily workflows truly need push notifications and mobile-first CRM actions.
