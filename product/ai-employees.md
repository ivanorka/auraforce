# AI Employees

AI employees are the product language that makes AuraForce AI easier to sell.

Clients do not need to understand agent orchestration, model routing or automation architecture. They understand roles.

## Core Idea

An AI employee is an installable role with a dedicated GUI, clear permissions and measurable business outcomes.

Each AI employee should include:

- role and job description
- domain template
- allowed actions
- scripts and playbooks
- tone of voice
- knowledge base access
- integrations
- permissions
- usage limits
- escalation rules
- human handoff rules
- audit trail
- success metrics

## GUI Principle

Each AI employee should have a simple control surface:

- what it is allowed to do
- which data it can access
- which scripts it follows
- when it escalates to a human
- how much it has used
- how well it is performing

This GUI is the difference between a generic chatbot and a business-operating AI teammate.

## First AI Employees

### AI Receptionist

Answers every inquiry, captures context, identifies intent and routes the lead.

### AI Sales Rep

Qualifies prospects, follows up, pushes next action and updates CRM status.

### AI Leasing Assistant

Handles rental inquiries, availability, viewing requests and reminders.

### AI Meeting Assistant

Creates notes, summaries, action items and follow-up tasks.

### AI Recruiter

Screens candidates, schedules interviews and tracks hiring stages.

### AI Call Centre Agent

Handles inbound and outbound call flows with strict scripts, consent rules and escalation.

## Long-Term Platform Value

The future marketplace is not just a template library. It is a catalogue of business roles that can be installed into a company workspace, configured per domain and controlled by humans.
