# Investment Thesis

## One-Liner

AuraForce AI is a Dubai-rooted AI command platform that helps high-value service businesses turn every inquiry into a managed sales process through installable AI employees.

## The Problem

Businesses pay for leads, then lose value through slow response, inconsistent follow-up and fragmented tools.

In real estate, inquiries move through WhatsApp, calls, email, websites, portals, spreadsheets and calendars. Owners often cannot see which leads were answered, qualified, booked, followed up or recovered.

The gap is not another CRM. The gap is instant, intelligent execution.

## The Solution

AuraForce AI combines CRM, unified inbox, appointment booking, workflow automation, analytics and AI employees into one command platform.

The first commercial package is an AI lead desk for real estate:

- AI Receptionist answers every inquiry.
- AI Sales Rep qualifies and follows up.
- AI Leasing Assistant handles rental viewing flows.
- Humans take over high-value decisions through designed handoff.
- Owners see response speed, qualified leads, booked viewings and AI performance.

## Why Dubai

Dubai is a strong launch market because the national AI narrative, real estate velocity and business digitization are converging. The product language also fits Dubai: premium, high-trust, concierge onboarding and clear operational ROI.

## Why Real Estate First

Real estate gives the clearest first wedge:

- lead value is high
- response speed matters
- WhatsApp is central
- appointments are measurable
- agency owners understand missed-lead cost
- AI receptionist and AI sales assistant are easy to sell

## Business Model

AuraForce should never hide AI costs inside unlimited promises.

Revenue should come from:

- platform subscription
- setup and onboarding fee
- usage-based margin
- premium AI employee packs
- later enterprise and vertical templates

Usage should meter AI tokens, model routing, voice minutes, call recordings, WhatsApp, SMS, email, storage and premium integrations.

## Competitive Edge

The moat is operational packaging:

- vertical-first workflow templates
- AI employees instead of abstract modules
- usage wallet and cost control
- multi-tenant SaaS architecture
- Dubai premium positioning
- human handoff by design
- domain-specific scripts, permissions and success metrics
- controlled AI improvement through human-approved knowledge updates

## Recommended Initial Raise

Raise `$350k-$500k`.

A leaner MVP may be possible for less, especially with offshore or hybrid AI-assisted development. The stronger investor recommendation is still `$350k-$500k` because AuraForce must fund product build, pilots, integrations, AI/voice usage, onboarding, design, legal/compliance and enough iteration time to prove paid demand.

Use of funds:

- 60% product and engineering
- 15% AI, voice, cloud and infrastructure buffer
- 15% Dubai pilots and go-to-market
- 10% legal, compliance, design and contingency

## Milestone Target

The round should create a fundable or revenue-supported next stage by delivering:

- real estate MVP
- 3-5 paying or committed pilot agencies
- working AI receptionist and AI sales workflows
- measurable response-time and booking improvements
- clear usage economics
- packaged pricing and onboarding
