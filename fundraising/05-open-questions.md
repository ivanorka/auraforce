# Open Questions For Ivan And Lewis

These are the questions that will make the investment story sharper.

## Founder And Company

- Who will be the official founder or founding team?
- Will the company be UAE-based from the start?
- What is the preferred fundraising structure: equity, SAFE, convertible note or strategic partnership?
- Is there already a target valuation or investor expectation?
- What is Lewis bringing: capital, market access, clients, product vision, operations or all of these?
- What is Ivan / ORKA / TrueForce bringing: build team, product leadership, technical execution, design, AI architecture or venture studio role?

## Market And Pilots

- Which 3-5 Dubai real estate agencies can be approached first?
- Does Lewis already have warm access to brokerage owners or developers?
- What is the strongest pain point from real conversations: missed WhatsApp leads, call handling, follow-up, booking, reporting or staff discipline?
- What would make an agency say yes to a pilot within one meeting?
- What is the minimum pilot fee they would realistically pay?

## Product

- Which channels must be in MVP: WhatsApp, email, web chat, phone calls?
- Should AI voice be in Phase 1 or Phase 2?
- Which calendar system matters first?
- What exact handoff flow should exist between AI and human agents?
- What must the owner dashboard show every morning?
- Which GUI screens should each AI employee have?

## Commercial Model

- What setup fee feels acceptable in Dubai real estate?
- Should first pilots be paid, discounted or free with written LOI?
- Which plan should be the flagship: Growth, Pro or Enterprise?
- What usage items should be included in the base package?
- What should be sold as premium AI employee packs?

## Brand And Web

- Is AuraForce AI the name Lewis wants to keep?
- Should the brand feel more luxury real estate, AI command centre or enterprise SaaS?
- Do we want a public landing page now or a private investor/client page first?
- What screenshots should we prioritize: dashboard, unified inbox, AI employee builder, pipeline, booking, usage wallet?
