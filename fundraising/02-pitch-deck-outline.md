# Pitch Deck Outline

This outline builds from the existing 14-page PDF and keeps the deck investor-focused.

## 1. Cover

Headline: AuraForce AI

Subline: AI Command Platform for Modern Business

Message: A premium Dubai-rooted SaaS concept helping companies capture leads, answer instantly, qualify prospects, book appointments and scale through installable AI employees.

## 2. Problem

The gap is not another CRM. The gap is instant, intelligent business execution.

Show fragmented tools: WhatsApp, calls, email, spreadsheets, calendars and portals.

## 3. Why Now

Dubai AI ambition, real estate velocity and business digitization are converging.

Keep sources visible but not overcrowded:

- UAE AI Strategy 2031
- Dubai real estate transaction momentum
- Dubai Economic Agenda D33
- regional AI infrastructure momentum

## 4. Solution

AuraForce AI is a command platform: unified communication, AI employees, workflow automation, CRM, usage wallet and executive intelligence.

## 5. First Wedge

Real estate first.

Simple flow:

Lead arrives -> AI answers -> qualifies -> books viewing -> follows up -> human closes.

## 6. Product Modules

Frame modules as business capabilities:

- Core SaaS
- CRM + Leads
- Unified Inbox
- AI Assistants
- Booking + Calendar
- Automation
- Analytics
- Usage Wallet
- Industry Templates

## 7. AI Employees

Make this the signature slide.

AI employees are role-based agents with GUI control, scripts, permissions, knowledge, integrations, limits, escalation rules and success metrics.

Examples:

- AI Receptionist
- AI Sales Rep
- AI Leasing Assistant
- AI Meeting Assistant
- AI Recruiter
- AI Call Centre Agent

## 8. Business Model

Margin-protected model:

- platform subscription
- setup and onboarding fee
- usage-based revenue
- premium AI employee packs

## 9. Go-To-Market

Target buyer:

Real estate agency owner, brokerage founder, sales director, premium property consultant.

Launch motion:

Pilot 3-5 agencies -> refine scripts -> package pricing -> expand through referrals, developer partnerships and agency networks.

## 10. Roadmap

Phase 1: `$150k-$300k`, real estate MVP, 3-5 months.

Phase 2: `+$150k-$350k`, stronger AI platform, 3-5 additional months.

Phase 3: `+$300k-$800k`, multi-niche expansion, 6-12 additional months.

## 11. Competitive Edge

The moat is operational packaging:

- vertical-first templates
- AI employee marketplace
- metered cost control
- multi-tenant architecture
- Dubai premium positioning
- human handoff by design

## 12. Investment Logic

Recommended initial raise: `$350k-$500k`.

Enough to build MVP, run pilots and avoid stopping before commercial proof.

## 13. Next Steps

Reduce risk and make the product feel inevitable:

- validate name and domains
- launch one-page landing page
- freeze MVP scope
- define technical architecture
- create pilot package
- build web/prototype/screens

## 14. Sources

List internal brief and market sources. Keep financial estimates marked as rough planning ranges pending final scope, provider costs, integrations and legal review.
