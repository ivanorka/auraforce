# Fundraising Plan

## Objective

Raise `$350k-$500k` to build a commercial real estate MVP, run Dubai pilots and produce proof that AuraForce AI can become a larger AI command platform.

## Round Narrative

This is not funding a broad feature list. It is funding one disciplined wedge with platform upside.

The first raise should answer four investor questions:

1. Can the team ship a working AI-led real estate sales product?
2. Will agencies pay for it?
3. Can the system show measurable response, booking and follow-up improvement?
4. Can the same platform core expand into new verticals?

## Use Of Funds

- Product and engineering: 60%
- AI, voice, cloud and infrastructure buffer: 15%
- Dubai pilots and go-to-market: 15%
- Legal, compliance, design and contingency: 10%

## 90-Day Fundraising Workplan

### Days 1-15

- freeze investor one-liner and positioning
- validate name and domains
- prepare pilot package
- identify 20-30 target investor or strategic partner conversations
- define MVP screen list and demo narrative

### Days 16-45

- create landing page
- create polished pitch deck
- build clickable prototype or visual screens
- prepare investor Q&A
- secure 2-3 pilot letters of interest if possible

### Days 46-90

- start investor conversations
- run pilot discovery calls with agencies
- refine pricing and package
- update deck with market feedback
- negotiate funding structure

## Best Investor Targets

- Dubai/UAE angel investors with real estate or technology exposure
- real estate brokerage owners who can also become pilot customers
- strategic investors connected to property developers
- B2B SaaS investors comfortable with AI usage economics
- operators with call centre, CRM, WhatsApp automation or property portal experience

## What Makes The Round Stronger

- 3-5 named pilot agencies
- signed letters of intent
- prototype screens that feel premium and real
- clear MVP quote and build plan
- usage economics with margin assumptions
- founder/operator story from Lewis and Ivan
- early technical architecture showing multi-tenancy and usage wallet
